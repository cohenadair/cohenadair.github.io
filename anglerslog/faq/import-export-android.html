Anglers' Log allows you to backup your data to your favorite cloud storage app, assuming said app is installed on your device.
Note! For any errors, or if you think your import or export didn't work property please file a bug report.
Exporting:
  1. On your device, open Anglers' Log and go to Settings.
  2. Tap "Export" and "Export" again.
  3. Wait for the exporting process to finish. This may take several minutes depending on how the amount of data you have.
  4. When exporting is complete you will be given your available transfer options.
  5. Tap the backup option you want and follow the prompts for that application.
  6. Your backup .zip file will now be uploaded to the selected cloud folder.
Importing:
  1. On your device, open Anglers' Log and go to Settings.
  2. Select your backup file. If the file is "greyed out", please file a bug report.
  3. Tap "Import"
  4. Wait for the import process to complete. This may take several minutes depending on how much data you have.

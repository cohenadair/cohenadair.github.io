It is likely that when you setup your new device you restored it from an iCloud backup. If this is the case you probably already have Anglers' Log installed along with your entries.
If you didn't restore it from an iCloud backup you have a couple options:
  1. Use Anglers' Log built in expoting and importing feature (recommended).
  2. Backup your old device and restore it on your new device using Apple's Restore or set up your iOS device from iCloud tutorial.

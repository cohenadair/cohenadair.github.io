Anglers' Log allows you to backup your entries to your favorite cloud storage app, assuming said app is installed on your device.
Note! For any errors see Importing and Exporting Errors.
Exporting:
  1. On your device, open Anglers' Log and go to Settings.
  2. Tap "Export" and "Export" again.
  3. Wait for the exporting process to finish. This may take several minutes depending on how many entries you have.
  4. When exporting is complete you will be given your available cloud options.
  5. Tap the backup option you want.
  6. You will be presented with your cloud backup folder.
  7. Tap "Export to this location", or "Save Here" (actual text varies).
  8. Your backup .zip file will now be uploaded to the selected cloud folder.
Importing:
  1. On your device, open Anglers' Log and go to Settings.
  2. Tap "Import" and "Import" again.
  3. Tap the backup option that contains your backup .zip file (i.e. Google Drive or Dropbox).
  4. You will be presented with your cloud backup folder.
  5. Select your backup file. If the file is "greyed out", see Importing and Exporting Errors.
  6. You will be notified when importing is complete. This may take several minutes depending on how many entries you have.
